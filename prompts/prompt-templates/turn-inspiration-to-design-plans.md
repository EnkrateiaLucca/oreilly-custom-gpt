# Turn Inspiration into Design Plans

## Source
https://claude.com/resources/use-case/turn-inspiration-to-design-plans

## Template Prompt

I'm planning a {PROJECT_TYPE|home renovation,interior redesign,office makeover,room refresh} for my {SPACE|living room,bedroom,kitchen,office,entire home}. I've saved {NUMBER} design inspiration images that show the style I'm drawn to.

Please help me:

**1. Analyze Design Inspiration:**
- Review my inspiration images and identify the {STYLE_ELEMENTS|aesthetic style,color palette,materials,patterns,lighting approach}
- Define the overall design direction: {STYLE_NAME|Modern minimalist,Scandinavian,Industrial,Mid-century modern,Bohemian,Traditional}
- Highlight consistent themes across the images

**2. Research and Compile Options:**
Research {ITEM_CATEGORIES|furniture,lighting fixtures,textiles,decor,flooring,paint colors} that match this aesthetic across {BUDGET_TIERS|budget,mid-range,premium,luxury} price tiers:
- {TIER_1|Budget-friendly} options ({PRICE_RANGE_1|under $500,under $1000})
- {TIER_2|Mid-range} options ({PRICE_RANGE_2|$500-$2000,$1000-$3000})
- {TIER_3|Premium} options ({PRICE_RANGE_3|$2000+,$3000+,investment pieces})

**3. Create Comprehensive Documentation:**
Format findings as a {OUTPUT_FORMAT|detailed spreadsheet,interactive workbook,formatted PDF,presentation} including:

**Style Guide Section:**
- Color palette with {COLOR_FORMAT|hex codes,paint brand names,Pantone numbers}
- Material specifications and textures
- Design principles and key characteristics
- Mood board reference page

**Product Database:**
Organize by category ({CATEGORIES|Seating,Tables,Storage,Lighting,Textiles,Accessories}) with:
- Product name and {VENDOR_INFO|retailer,direct link,local availability}
- Dimensions and specifications
- Price comparison across tiers
- Lead times and availability
- Quality indicators and reviews

**Financial Planning:**
- {COST_ANALYSIS|Total cost by tier,Cost per category,Phased budget options}
- {ROI_PROJECTIONS|Investment value comparison,Long-term cost analysis,Cost-per-use calculations}
- {RECOMMENDATIONS|Best value picks,Splurge vs. save suggestions,Priority ranking}

**Implementation Guide:**
- {TIMELINE|Phased approach,All-at-once plan,Flexible timeline}
- {SOURCING_STRATEGY|Where to buy,When to buy,Alternatives}
- {PRACTICAL_NOTES|Measurement requirements,Installation needs,Compatibility checks}

## Use Case Description

This template helps transform design inspiration into actionable renovation plans. Claude analyzes reference images to identify aesthetic preferences, researches materials and products at multiple price points, and creates comprehensive documentation organizing findings with cost comparisons and investment recommendations to support decision-making.

## Variables Explanation

- **PROJECT_TYPE**: Type of design project being undertaken
- **SPACE**: Specific area being renovated or redesigned
- **NUMBER**: How many inspiration images are being analyzed
- **STYLE_ELEMENTS**: Aspects of design to identify
- **STYLE_NAME**: Named design style or aesthetic
- **ITEM_CATEGORIES**: Types of items to research
- **BUDGET_TIERS**: Price level categories
- **TIER_1-3**: Specific budget tier names
- **PRICE_RANGE_1-3**: Price ranges for each tier
- **OUTPUT_FORMAT**: How to format the final documentation
- **COLOR_FORMAT**: How to specify colors
- **CATEGORIES**: Product organization categories
- **VENDOR_INFO**: Shopping information to include
- **COST_ANALYSIS**: Financial breakdown approach
- **ROI_PROJECTIONS**: Investment analysis to include
- **RECOMMENDATIONS**: Decision-support information
- **TIMELINE**: Implementation scheduling approach
- **SOURCING_STRATEGY**: Purchasing guidance
- **PRACTICAL_NOTES**: Additional practical considerations

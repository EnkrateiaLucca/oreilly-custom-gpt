## 🏢 Enterprise & Security

Both platforms offer SSO (SAML/OIDC), SCIM provisioning, audit logging, and admin consoles suitable for regulated environments. Claude leans on Constitutional AI and deep Microsoft 365 integration (Teams, SharePoint, Outlook); ChatGPT emphasizes broader third-party connector support and enterprise-tier browsing/research agents. Exact plan names, prices, and quotas change frequently — check each vendor's current pricing page before quoting specifics to a class.

## 💡 Conclusion

Both platforms are exceptionally capable with different strengths.

**Claude excels at:** professional coding workflows, handling massive contexts, enterprise Microsoft integrations, transparent AI reasoning.

**ChatGPT excels at:** creative image generation, voice interaction, customizable GPTs, ecosystem breadth.

**The best choice depends on your specific needs:**
- **Developers/Engineers**: Claude Code + Opus 4.8 or Sonnet 5
- **Creative Professionals**: ChatGPT for `gpt-image-2` + Advanced Voice
- **Enterprise Teams**: both offer strong options — Claude for Microsoft-centric shops, ChatGPT for broader third-party integrations

Both platforms continue to innovate rapidly. Treat exact pricing, quotas, and tier names as subject to change — verify against the vendor's current docs before presenting to students.
